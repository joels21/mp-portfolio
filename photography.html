Lowry Theatre

Here is an image of the Lowry outlet and the two bridges that go over Salford quays, I took this picture because i like how the bridges are symmetrical with the main part of the picture in the middle of them both.

Media city

Here is an image of Media city, I took this image becasue it is the modern part of Salford quays, I like this image because it is took from the other side of the the Quays and the water creates a nice reflection to make the image more visually appealing.
